<ul>
  <li>Designed a controlled-field hybrid experiment to evaluate how short-term investment outcomes shape judgment precision and risk behavior.</li>
  <li>Implemented randomized feedback sequences and collected real-world account aggregates to measure calibration, aggression, and attribution patterns.</li>
  <li>Conducted longitudinal analysis revealing dynamic shifts from emotionally driven reactions to experience-based adaptive learning.</li>
  <li>Identified mechanisms linking recency effects, overconfidence, and bounded learning; developed low-cost behavioral interventions for trading platforms.</li>
