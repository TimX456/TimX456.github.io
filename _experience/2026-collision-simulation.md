  <li>Integrated theoretical derivations with large-scale numerical experiments to validate convergence properties and reveal nonlinear growth patterns underlying the classic Birthday Paradox.</li>
  <li>Reframed the paradox as a broader class of collision probability modeling problems, enabling practical insights into hashing algorithms, data deduplication pipelines, and large-scale distributed infrastructure.</li>
  <li>Demonstrated how probabilistic modeling and simulation can inform system design decisions where collision risks directly impact performance, reliability, and storage efficiency.</li>
